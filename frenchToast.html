Ingredients

  • 1.5 dl Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 ml Salt
  • 4 thick bread slices
  • 2 tbs butter for frying
  • 1 ml cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Whisk together milk, eggs, salt, cinnamon and sugar in a bowl.

  2. Heat a frying pan with butter

  3. Dip 4 thick slices of bread into the batter and fry them on both sides until you get a nice crispy and golden brown surface

  4. Top your French toast with some mixed berries and a scoop of ice cream.
